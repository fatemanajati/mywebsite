Becoming a Modest & Beauty Influencer!

When I decided that I wanted to be an influencer, I started to have interests in different fields of it, especially when it came to beauty, it fascinated me the most. I love to wear makeup, play with different colors and create looks as well as teach my followers a few tricks and good products that I've used for each unique look! My platform is what has given me the opportunity to display these skills and having an audience to share it with is the best part about it all.

The Power Of Makeup

Beauty is something that we all have in our own unique way and makeup was made to enhance these beauty marks and not hide them! That has been my biggest inspiration going into this. Sometimes in the industry, things can get a little out of hand, being that some influencers use makeup to make them a completely different person instead of using a little bit just to beautify their features. This has an adverse an impact on young girls because they are easily influenced by their favorite "idols" that they see or follow and will feel free to do the same, nonetheless it is absolutely not what parents want for their children or what the beauty industry should be portrayed as.

Yellow Sunshine

I usually tend to go towards the trends of what is new in the beauty world and try to create those looks and if not, add more to it if I have any creative ideas which makes it alot more fun for me! Ofcourse, this process takes time and needs a stable hand and loads of patience, but the result always looks flawless in the end. The picture above is one of my best-loved looks that I did a while back. This consisted of some light yellow and brown toned eyeshadows mixed with a little shimmer, topped with a white winged liner to complete the full look!

Cherry Pie

Another look I went for was a Red Cherry look which is my second favorite, I mixed together a few red and dark pink shades to create this look, topped with a Cranberry colored lipstick, Since I was wearing a maroon top underneath my jeans jacket, it matched the makeup look perfectly and got so many compliments about the color choice!

Gypsy Queen

The third look has more of a "Gypsy Queen" vibe to it, It consists of a shimmery dark blue eyeshadow on my eyelids with a black winged eyeliner, followed by a simple nude color lipstick. This look can be for a simple night out, maybe a little extra on the eyes but overall toned down with the skin colored lipstick.
